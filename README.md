Hi 👋 My name is Tomasz Bazelczuk

Software Developer
------------------

I have a lot of experience in developing various applications on the web, mobile, STB, Roku, and smart TV platforms. I always prioritize high quality and try to use cutting-edge technologies when possible. I am an open-minded person who is not afraid to take risks and embrace new challenges.
